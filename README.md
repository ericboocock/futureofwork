# Future of Work 🗺️

> Practical, steal-it-today agentic AI tips — for the people who keep telling me they haven't *quite* figured out how to make AI work for them.

I'm still surprised how often I talk to smart, capable people who haven't really embraced what AI can do for them. It's not their fault — the gap between *"I tried ChatGPT once"* and *"AI now runs parts of my day"* is wide, and nobody's handing out the map.

So this is the map.

Over the last six months I've gone all-in on AI, and it's helped me pull off things I genuinely didn't think were possible. This repo is where I'll share those wins one at a time — real, working setups you can copy into your own day, not theory.

No code required for most of them. Just paste, schedule, and let it run.

## How to use this repo

1. Open the tip you want from the table above.
2. Each tip has a short **what it does**, a **setup guide**, and a **copy-paste prompt**.
3. Paste, set the schedule, done. Tomorrow it runs on its own.

---

## Why "Future of Work"?

Because the highest-leverage way to use AI isn't asking it to do one task — it's wiring it into the *rhythms* of how you work, so the value compounds while you sleep. That's the shift these tips are about: moving from **reacting** to your day to **running** it.
